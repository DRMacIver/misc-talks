The theory in a nutshell

  1. An emotion word labels behaviour, not internal state.
  2. Feeling an emotion is some part of you wanting to behave that way.
  3. It wants this because it thinks that behaviour is a good idea.
  4. It may or may not be right.
  5. When it is not, you can often change the feeling.
  6. When it is, you can learn to act skillfully on it.
